--- n: 203 name: "Curse (Non-commulitive)" param1: "Penalty Value" param2: "Undetermined" bg1: 0 bg2: 0 bgee: 0 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 1 pstee: 0 --- This efect causes a penalty to all saving throws and to hit rolls in the amount that the Penalty Value parameter is set to. For every 1 of the penalty value, all saving throws get a penalty of 1, as well as all die throws and damage from the target. This cannot remove the 'Blessed' status from a target, but can effectively negate it.
