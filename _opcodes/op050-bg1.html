--- n: 50 name: "Colour: Glow by RGB (Brief)" param1: "RGB Colour" param2: "Speed" bg1: 1 bg2: 0 bgee: 0 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 0 pstee: 0 --- The targeted creature(s) entire animation glows the colour specified by the 'RGB Colour' field, at the speed specified by the (3rd byte of the) 'Speed' field.
The 'RGB Colour' field is handled as follows:
Second byte = Red (0-255)
Third byte = Green (0-255)
Fourth byte = Blue (0-255)
The 'Speed' field is handled as follows:
Third byte = Speed (0-255)
A speed of 0 does not pulsate. A speed of 1 is fastest, and a speed of 255 is slowest.
