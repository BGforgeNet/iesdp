--- n: 131 opname: "State: Chant" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Type" bg1: 0 bg2: 0 bgee: 0 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 1 pst: 0 pstee: 0 --- Applies the chant effect to the targeted creature(s), in the style specified by the 'Type' field. Chant affects the minimum damage caused by a weapon (up to the normal maximum damage of the weapon) and the attack roll modifier (up to a maximum of 20, excluding natural attack roll modifiers, e.g. high STR, invisibility etc) by 'Statistic Modifier' amount. The effect sets STATE_CHANT.
Known values for 'Type are:
0 Beneficial
1 Detrimenal
