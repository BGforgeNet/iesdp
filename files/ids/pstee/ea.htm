--- title: "PSTEE Identifiers : ea.ids" ---
ea.ids

Relates to offset 0x270 in the CRE file format.
For use with the ChangeEnemyAlly() action.
For use with the Allegiance() and AreaCheckAllegiance() triggers.

10

1 INANIMATE
E.g. Gears in the Great Foundry of the Godsmen ("FOUNGEAR.CRE")

2 PC
Regular party members.

3 FAMILIAR
Familiars of mages.

4 ALLY

128 NEUTRAL
Creatures that are neutral (blue selection circle).

255 ENEMY
Creatures that are hostile to the party and allied creatures (red selection circle).
